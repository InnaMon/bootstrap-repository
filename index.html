About Me

Igor Monjoseph, CPA, CMA (Both inactive) is a consultant at Clarity BAI and is passionate in helping Organizations thrive by providing accounting services and online, interactive reporting tools to help fuel better business decisions. With numerous years of experience in a multitude of business settings he believes in unlocking value for business owners through financial clarity and providing crucial information and insights to make more informed choices.

Igor graduated from the Carlson School of Management with a Masters of Accountancy degree and obtained the CPA and CMA licensures. He started his career working for CBRE, a Fortune 500 Company. Through his time there, he was involved in starting a new department for the Company and providing accounting services/controls to the Organization. After his tenure there, Igor worked for Boulay, a public accounting firm, providing auditing and accounting services to mid-size businesses across the US. This is where he developed his passion for helping business owners achieve even better results through financial insight. After this, he worked for Cargill as a Senior Management Information and Decision Support Analyst. He was tasked with helping his supported businesses make better and more data driven decisions by building reporting tools/solutions for the business teams and providing consul on appropriate courses of action to achieve greater results.

Through this myriad of business experiences he realized that his passion was in serving as a partner to leaders and decision makers to enable them to guide their respective Organizations to greater heights by both releasing them from the effort of maintaining financial systems and providing the necessary information to make more informed and more data driven decisions
